Peace of Mind for Students

ResQme has given me peace of mind knowing that in case of any medical emergency, my important details like blood group and allergies are easily available through my QR code. It makes me feel more secure both on campus and when traveling.”

Ananya

Student

Support for first responders

“During emergencies, every second counts. With ResQme, I can quickly scan a patient’s QR code and instantly access vital information like medical conditions and emergency contacts.

Matt Brandon

Frequent Traveller
